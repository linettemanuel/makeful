What happens when you get in touch?

We read every message ourselves. You will not hit an automated system or wait weeks for a reply. We will come back to you within 2 working days with a personal response.

If you are a school coordinator or teacher, we will send you a full info pack covering program options, pricing, what we need from you and how to get started. If you have specific questions, ask them directly in the message and we will answer each one.

Not sure where to start?

Here are the three ways schools work with Makeful.

Each pathway is designed for a different context, from training your own teachers to running a full year-long program for students.

school

Teacher Training

A self-paced online program that equips your teachers with the skills and materials to run FashionTech STEAM themselves. A physical kit is included.

Learn More
wb_sunny

Summer Camp

A complete 14-day FashionTech STEAM camp licensed to your school. Makeful trains your staff and provides all hardware and materials. You run it. Students make something real.

Learn More
calendar_month

Full-Year Enrichment

Small groups, sessions every two weeks led by Makeful instructors, and a full portfolio by the end of the year. Hardware delivered to your school and fully covered by Makeful.
