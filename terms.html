Programs & Services

Makeful offers three core program formats. All programs combine FashionTech design, 3D printing and STEAM education into hands-on learning experiences.

Teacher Professional Development

A self-paced online program equipping teachers with the skills, confidence, and materials to bring FashionTech STEAM into their own classrooms. Includes 25 modules, 70+ video tutorials, inspirational talks, mentoring, and a complete 3D-printed fashion kit with materials. Delivered via the Esmerise platform.

Summer Camp Program (School License)

A complete 14-day program licensed to schools for delivery by their own trained staff. Makeful does not send instructors to schools. Instead, Makeful trains the school's designated staff members to deliver the program confidently and effectively. Makeful provides the full program structure, curriculum, training, loaned 3D printers and all materials. Schools provide the venue, trained staff, supervision and eligible students. Maximum class sizes apply as specified in the program agreement.

Short-Term Programs

Any other short-term programs, workshops or holiday programs offered by Makeful follow the same model as the Summer Camp: Makeful trains the school's own staff to deliver the program. Makeful does not deploy its own instructors on-site. Trained school staff run all in-person sessions, supported by Makeful's curriculum and materials.

Full-Year Enrichment Program

An academic-year program running bi-weekly sessions in small groups (maximum 8 students). Students build a complete project portfolio over the year. Sessions are delivered online by Makeful-qualified instructors, with the school's designated guardian present at all times. Hardware is provided, delivered to the school, and managed by Makeful throughout.

Specific program dates, group sizes, deliverables and schedules are confirmed in writing at the point of enrollment. These form part of the Agreement and supersede any general descriptions on our website.

Enrollment & Registration

Enrollment is confirmed when Makeful issues a written confirmation by email, following receipt of a completed registration form and, where applicable, payment of any required deposit. No place on a program is guaranteed until written confirmation is issued.

School enrollment

Schools enrolling students into Makeful programs take responsibility for:

  • Obtaining parental or guardian consent for each student to participate in the program and to have their basic data (name, age group, email address) processed by Makeful.
  • Ensuring that enrolled students meet any stated age or prerequisite requirements for the specific program.
  • Providing accurate student information at the point of registration, including any medical conditions, disabilities, or needs that may require Makeful to make reasonable adjustments.
  • Informing Makeful promptly of any changes to enrolled student details.

Individual enrollment

Where individuals (teachers or parents) register directly, the registering adult takes on the responsibilities above in respect of any minors in their care.

Waiting lists

Where a program is fully subscribed, schools may be placed on a waiting list. Makeful will notify waiting-list applicants of availability as soon as it arises. Being on a waiting list does not constitute enrollment and carries no financial obligation.

Fees & Payment

Program fees are as stated in the relevant program offer or proposal communicated to the school at the time of enrollment. All fees are exclusive of VAT unless otherwise stated. VAT will be applied at the applicable rate where required by law.

Payment schedule

  • Deposit A non-refundable deposit (amount confirmed in the program proposal) is due upon enrollment to secure the school's place. The program is not confirmed until the deposit is received.
  • Balance The remaining balance is due no later than 14 days before the program start date, unless an alternative payment schedule has been agreed in writing.
  • Full-year programs Payment for full-year programs may be made in termly instalments as agreed at enrollment. Instalment schedules and due dates will be confirmed in writing.
  • Teacher development Online teacher development courses are payable in full at point of purchase before access to the platform is granted.

Late payment

Where payment is not received by the due date, Makeful reserves the right to suspend or withhold program access until the outstanding balance is cleared. Makeful may charge statutory interest on overdue amounts at a rate of 8% above the Czech National Bank base rate. We will always contact you first to resolve payment issues before exercising these rights.

Invoicing

Makeful will issue a VAT invoice (or equivalent tax document) for all program fees. Schools requiring specific invoicing formats or purchase order references should notify Makeful at the point of registration.

Cancellation & Refunds

Cancellation by the school

  • More than 30 days before start Full refund of any payments made, less the non-refundable deposit.
  • 15–30 days before start 50% of the total program fee is due (or retained if already paid). The deposit is non-refundable.
  • Fewer than 15 days before start The full program fee is due and non-refundable. Makeful will have committed significant resources in preparation.
  • During the program No refund is available for program sessions already delivered. Where a full-year program is cancelled mid-way, fees for completed terms are non-refundable; upcoming term fees will not be charged.

Cancellation by Makeful

Makeful reserves the right to cancel a program where minimum enrollment thresholds are not met, where circumstances beyond our reasonable control prevent delivery, or where a school's conduct is in breach of these terms. In the event of cancellation by Makeful:

  • We will provide as much notice as possible.
  • A full refund of all fees paid will be issued within 14 days, including the deposit.
  • We will endeavour to offer an alternative date or program format wherever possible.

Individual student withdrawal

Where a school withdraws an individual student from a group program after enrollment, no partial refund is available unless the withdrawal occurs more than 30 days before the program starts. Makeful will consider exceptional circumstances, such as serious illness, on a case-by-case basis.

All cancellations must be submitted in writing by email to [email protected]. The cancellation date is the date Makeful receives and acknowledges the written notice.

Hardware & Equipment

Makeful is fully responsible for all hardware.

All 3D printers, tools and equipment provided for Makeful programs are owned, maintained, insured and fully managed by Makeful. Schools and students are never responsible for the cost of hardware repair, replacement or failure, regardless of how damage occurs during normal program use.

Provision of hardware

For Summer Camp and Full-Year Enrichment programs, Makeful provides all necessary 3D printers and equipment on a loan basis for the duration of the program. Hardware is delivered to the school venue before the program start date and collected by Makeful after the program concludes.

All hardware provided is:

  • Fully tested and verified by Makeful before delivery.
  • Covered by Makeful's own insurance for the duration of the loan period.
  • Maintained and serviced by Makeful, schools do not need to arrange or pay for maintenance.
  • Replaced or repaired by Makeful at no additional cost to the school in the event of malfunction, breakdown, or normal wear during program use.

Materials and consumables

All filaments, fabrics, electronic components and consumable materials required for the program are included in the program fee and provided by Makeful. Schools are not required to source or purchase any materials separately unless a specific optional extension has been agreed.

School responsibilities for hardware

Whilst Makeful takes full responsibility for hardware, schools agree to:

  • Store hardware securely in a designated, lockable space when not in use during the program period.
  • Ensure hardware is only operated by enrolled students and Makeful-certified instructors during supervised program sessions.
  • Not move hardware between locations without Makeful's prior written consent.
  • Notify Makeful immediately (within 24 hours) of any hardware theft, loss, or accidental damage caused outside of normal program activity.

Intentional damage or theft: In the exceptional event of hardware theft or intentional damage caused by the school's own negligence, for example failure to secure equipment as agreed, Makeful reserves the right to recover the reasonable cost of replacement from the school. This does not apply to accidental breakage, normal wear and tear, or any hardware malfunction arising from the equipment itself.

Hardware for Teacher Professional Development

The Teacher Professional Development program includes a physical 3D-printed fashion kit and materials shipped directly to the registered teacher. This kit is provided as part of the course fee and becomes the property of the teacher upon receipt. Makeful is responsible for safe delivery. Once received and acknowledged, the kit is the teacher's responsibility.

School Obligations

To enable Makeful to deliver a safe, effective, and enjoyable program, schools agree to the following:

Venue and facilities

  • Provide a suitable, dedicated workspace for program delivery, with adequate power outlets (minimum one per 3D printer), reasonable ventilation, tables at an appropriate height, and sufficient space for materials and student work.
  • Ensure the workspace is accessible to Makeful instructors before and after sessions for setup and pack-down (minimum 30 minutes before and after each session).
  • Provide reliable Wi-Fi access in the workspace for online resources, digital design tools, and program platform access.

Supervision and safeguarding

  • Designate one or more qualified staff members to be trained by Makeful for Summer Camp and short-term program delivery. Designated staff must be available for all Makeful training sessions before the program begins.
  • Ensure that a school guardian, a school staff member with valid DBS (or equivalent local safeguarding clearance), is present and actively supervising during all online Makeful sessions for the Full-Year Enrichment Program.
  • Follow the school's own safeguarding and child protection policies throughout all program activity.
  • Inform Makeful of any relevant safeguarding considerations relating to individual students prior to the program start.

Health, safety and conduct

  • Ensure students comply with Makeful's safety guidelines for 3D printer operation and materials handling, as communicated by the Makeful instructor at program induction.
  • Notify Makeful of any known allergies or sensitivities to materials that may be used during the program (e.g. certain plastics, adhesives).
  • Maintain appropriate student behaviour during sessions. Where persistent behavioural issues disrupt the program, the school should address these using its own disciplinary procedures.

Promotion and communications

  • Promptly communicate Makeful's program information, schedules, and any required pre-work to enrolled students and parents.
  • Notify Makeful of any planned school events, closures, or timetable changes that may affect agreed program sessions with at least 7 days' notice.

Photography and showcasing

Makeful may wish to photograph, film, or document student work and program sessions for use in our marketing materials, website, social media, press coverage, and educational publications. We will always:

Safeguarding & Conduct

The safety and wellbeing of young people is Makeful's highest priority. We hold ourselves and our partners to a high standard.

Makeful's safeguarding commitment

  • All Makeful online instructors working with minors have completed safeguarding training appropriate to working with young people in an online environment.
  • Makeful instructors never communicate with minor students one-to-one or without school supervision. All communication channels are structured so that a school guardian is always present or copied in.
  • Any safeguarding concern arising during a program will be immediately reported to the school's designated safeguarding lead, and to relevant authorities where legally required.

Communication with students, strict rules

Makeful operates a zero-tolerance policy on unsupervised communication between Makeful instructors and minor students. The following rules apply without exception:

  • Email Any email communication from a Makeful instructor to a student must always include the school guardian in copy (CC). Instructors will never email a minor student directly without a school guardian copied.
  • Student forum Written communication between instructors and students takes place in the Esmerise platform's student forum, which is publicly visible to all enrolled participants and the school guardian. There are no private messages between instructors and individual students.
  • Live online sessions All live sessions are conducted with the school guardian present in the call. Makeful instructors will not proceed with a live session if no school guardian is present.
  • No private channels Makeful instructors will never contact students via personal social media, direct messaging apps, personal email, or any channel outside those listed above.

Schools are responsible for ensuring that their designated guardian is present and available for all scheduled online sessions. If a guardian cannot attend a session, the school must notify Makeful at least 24 hours in advance. Makeful reserves the right to reschedule the session rather than proceed without guardian supervision.

Expected conduct, students

Students are expected to treat equipment, materials, and fellow participants with care and respect. Makeful instructors have the right to pause or remove a student from an online session where their behaviour poses a safety risk or significantly disrupts the program. The school guardian will be notified immediately in such cases.

Expected conduct, adults

All adults involved in Makeful programs, school staff, guardians, and Makeful instructors alike, are expected to model respectful, positive behaviour and to support the program's ethos of curiosity, creativity, and joy.

Online safety

Students access the Esmerise platform using their assigned credentials. Schools are responsible for ensuring students access the platform in a supervised, school-managed environment and that login credentials are not shared. All student forum activity is visible to the school guardian and to Makeful at all times.

Changes to Programs

Makeful may occasionally need to make changes to program delivery. We distinguish between minor adjustments and material changes:

Minor adjustments

Makeful may make reasonable adjustments to session schedules, the order of curriculum modules, specific project briefs, or supporting materials without prior notice, provided these do not affect the overall program quality or duration. We will communicate such changes to enrolled schools as soon as practicable.

Material changes

A material change is one that significantly alters the program's nature, duration, location, or cost. In the event of a material change, Makeful will:

  • Give at least 14 days' written notice.
  • Explain the reason for the change clearly.
  • Offer the school the option to accept the change or receive a full refund of all fees paid.

Curriculum evolution

Makeful continuously improves its curriculum based on feedback and new developments in FashionTech. Schools enrolling in future program years should expect to encounter updated or new content, projects and materials. Core program commitments, including duration, hardware provision and instructor quality, remain constant.

Termination

Either party may terminate the Agreement in the following circumstances:

Termination by the school

Schools may terminate the Agreement by written notice at any time. Cancellation fees as set out in Section 5 will apply. Upon termination, all Makeful hardware must be made available for collection within 5 working days.

Termination by Makeful

Makeful may terminate the Agreement immediately and without financial penalty to the school in the following circumstances:

  • The school fails to pay outstanding fees within 30 days of a formal overdue notice.
  • The school materially breaches these terms and fails to remedy the breach within 14 days of written notice.
  • Continuing the program would, in Makeful's reasonable assessment, place the safety or wellbeing of students or instructors at risk.

In all cases of termination by Makeful, the school will receive a proportional refund for any paid program sessions not yet delivered.

Effect of termination

Upon termination, all access to Makeful's online platform will be suspended, hardware must be returned to Makeful within 5 working days and any outstanding fees become immediately due. Sections 9 (Intellectual Property), 12 (Liability) and 15 (General Provisions) survive termination.
